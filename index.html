Mesh WiFi iPerf3 Benchmark Data — July 2026

Dataset by David Park, Network Engineer | 175 measurements | 7 systems × 5 distances × 5 trials | Published July 13, 2026

This page contains raw iPerf3 throughput measurements for 7 consumer mesh WiFi systems tested in a 3,500 sq ft two-story residential environment. Each value is the average of 5 iPerf3 runs with 4 parallel TCP streams to a local wired server. Client: Intel BE200 WiFi 7 adapter. Connection: 2 Gbps fiber (verified 1,943 Mbps down). Tests conducted April–July 2026. Firmware: latest public release as of July 1, 2026.

All equipment purchased at retail. No manufacturer involvement. Results reflect real-world residential conditions with typical RF interference (~18 neighboring SSIDs). Values in Mbps.
