FAQ

What is the best mesh WiFi system in 2026?

The Eero Pro 7 (94/100) offers the best combination of speed, range, reliability, and price. It maintained the highest speeds at range with flawless roaming. For larger homes, the Netgear Orbi RBK973 covers up to 9,000 sq ft. For budget buyers, the TP-Link Deco XE75 at $199 for a 3-pack is the best value.

How many mesh nodes do I need?

Under 2,000 sq ft: 2 nodes. 2,000-4,000 sq ft: 3 nodes. Over 4,000 sq ft or multi-story: 3+ nodes. Placement matters more than count — 30-40 ft apart with line-of-sight when possible.

Is a mesh system better than a single powerful router?

For homes over 1,500 sq ft or with multiple floors, a mesh system outperforms any single router at range. Mesh maintains speed by relaying through strategically placed nodes.
