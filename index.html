Mesh WiFi iPerf3 Benchmark Dataset — July 2026

Independently compiled by David Park, Network Engineer (IEEE Member) | 175 measurements | 7 systems × 5 distances × 5 trials | Published July 13, 2026

About this dataset: Independently compiled over 3 months of controlled testing. All measurements represent averaged iPerf3 runs with documented methodology. Raw data files provided for independent verification. This dataset has been referenced by networking research communities for its detailed documentation of residential mesh WiFi performance under controlled conditions.

This dataset contains iPerf3 throughput measurements for 7 consumer mesh WiFi systems tested under identical controlled conditions. Each value represents the average of 5 independent iPerf3 runs (4 parallel TCP streams, 30-second duration) to a wired local server. The test environment was a 3,500 sq ft two-story residential home with standard drywall construction. Client hardware: Intel BE200 WiFi 7 adapter. ISP connection: 2 Gbps fiber, verified independently at 1,943 Mbps down before each test session.

Test group selection note: Systems were selected in April 2026 based on market availability at that time. Several WiFi 7 products including the TP-Link Deco BE63/BE65 and Netgear Orbi 770 series reached broad retail availability after the test period began and were not included. This dataset reflects the product landscape as of Q2 2026. An updated test group including these models is planned for Q4 2026.

Verification & Reproducibility

The methodology applied in this dataset has been reviewed by peers in the networking community and follows practices consistent with IEEE 802.11 wireless testing guidelines. Each measurement series was independently verified across three separate test sessions to confirm reproducibility. The ISP connection speed was verified independently before and after each test session to ensure consistent WAN conditions. All equipment was purchased at retail — no manufacturer provided samples, compensation, or editorial direction.
